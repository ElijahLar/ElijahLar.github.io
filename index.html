Peppermint

Mentha piperita

Description

A sharp, minty, and refreshing scent that immediately awakens the senses. Peppermint has a clean, cool aroma that stimulates the mind and refreshes the spirit.

Mood & Emotional Effect

Alert, focused, and mentally refreshed. Peppermint activates the trigeminal nerve strongly, increasing alertness, attention, and cognitive performance.

Alertness Focus Cognitive Boost Refreshment

Scientific Benefit

Peppermint essential oil activates the trigeminal nerve, which has been shown in studies to significantly increase alertness, attention, and cognitive performance.

Experience Type

This scent supports morning journalling by sharpening mental clarity, increasing alertness, and preparing the mind for focused, productive writing.

Ginger

Zingiber officinale

Description

A warm, spicy, and awakening scent with earthy undertones. Ginger provides a gentle but effective stimulation that feels both grounding and energizing.

Mood & Emotional Effect

Focused, invigorated, and fatigue-resistant. As a mild trigeminal stimulant, ginger can improve focus, reduce mental fatigue, and provide gentle invigoration.

Focus Invigoration Fatigue Reduction Awakening

Scientific Benefit

Ginger acts as a mild trigeminal stimulant, with studies suggesting it can improve focus, reduce fatigue, and provide invigoration without overstimulation.

Experience Type

This scent supports morning journalling by reducing mental fatigue, improving concentration for detailed reflection, and providing gentle awakening energy.
